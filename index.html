It's a Secret - Be Free


It's a Secret is an application that allows the user to share whatever you want: Your adventures, lies, wishes and fantasies. Secret lets you take a picture (or choose one from your gallery) and publish it on your wall. The application uses your location to show you nearby posts from other people around you.

Be Free!

Remember, no one will know who you are, you are free to share whatever you want: Your adventures, lies, wishes and fantasies. Its a curious world. To create an account you only need 3 things: a username (emojis are allowed), an e-mail and a password. The application is totally anonymous.

Its a Secret is an anonymous way to be free, no one will know who you are. With a Double tap you can express how much you like something or someone. Remember, its a secret. Shhhhh!
