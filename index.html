Be Free Anonymous Yourself!

It's a Secret is an application that allows the user to interact and sharesecrets and photos or whatever you want. Secret lets you take a picture (or choose one from your gallery) and publish it on your wall. The application uses your location to show you nearby posts from other people around you. The application is totally anonymous.
