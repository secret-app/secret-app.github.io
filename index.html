It's a Secret - Be Free!

It's a Secret is an application that allows the user to share whatever you want: Your adventures, lies, wishes and fantasies. Secret lets you take a picture (or choose one from your gallery) and publish it on your wall. The application uses your location to show you nearby posts from other people around you.
